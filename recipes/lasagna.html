Lasagna Recipe

A delicious lasagna

This lasagna is made with tomato, flour and other ingredients

  • Tomato (1/2 kg)
  • Flour (1kg)
  • Heavy cream (1 lt)
  1. Cook the tomato in hot water for 20 min.
  2. Smash the tomato until it become a sauce
  3. Make the lasagna sheets with the flour
  4. Make the layers of the lasagna with the tomato sauce, the cream and the lasagna sheets
